Privacy Policy

App: Resurrection BSG · Provider: Resurrection Ministries · Last updated: 18 June 2026

Resurrection BSG (the “App”) is a private attendance-management tool used by the leaders and administrators of Resurrection Ministries Bible Study Groups (BSGs). This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. The App is intended for authorised group leaders and administrators only; it is not a consumer social app.

Information we collect

To record attendance for Bible study groups, the App processes the following data about group members, which is entered by authorised leaders/administrators:

  • Member details: name and (optionally) surname, mobile number, and address details (city/street) selected from admin-defined lists.
  • Photographs and face data: reference photos of members and group photos uploaded during meetings. From these images the App derives facial-recognition data (mathematical face templates / embeddings) which are used solely to recognise members and mark attendance.
  • Attendance records: which members were present or absent at a given meeting, and visitor entries pending review.
  • Account data for leaders/administrators: name, mobile number, and a securely hashed password used to sign in.

How we use the information

  • To identify members from photos and automatically mark meeting attendance.
  • To maintain group membership, attendance history, and basic reports for the ministry.
  • To authenticate leaders and administrators who sign in to the App.

We do not use this data for advertising, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

Facial recognition / biometric data

Face templates derived from member photos are used exclusively for attendance recognition within Resurrection Ministries. They are stored on the ministry’s own private server and are not shared with third parties. A member (or their guardian) may request that their photos and derived face data be deleted by contacting the ministry using the details below.

Data storage and security

Data is stored on a private server operated for Resurrection Ministries. Photographs are stored as files on the server; member and attendance records are stored in the ministry’s database. Access requires an authenticated leader or administrator account, passwords are stored only in hashed form, and connections to the server use encrypted HTTPS.

Data sharing

We do not share personal data or face data with third parties for their own purposes. Data is only processed by the infrastructure providers that host the ministry’s server, acting on the ministry’s behalf, and only as needed to operate the App.

Data retention and deletion

Member, photo, face, and attendance data are retained for as long as the member is part of a Bible study group, or until deletion is requested. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, contact us at the address below and we will act on the request.

Children

The App is operated by group leaders, not by members directly. Where data about a minor is recorded, it is provided and managed by the ministry/guardians. Requests concerning a minor’s data can be made using the contact details below.
